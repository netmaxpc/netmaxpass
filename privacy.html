Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy describes how NetMaxPass LLC (“Provider”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the NetMaxPass tooling and QuickBooks Online integrations (the “Service”).

1. Information we process

  • Account data from QuickBooks Online that you explicitly authorize via OAuth (e.g., chart of accounts, reports).
  • Operational logs for troubleshooting (timestamps, action types, and errors). No credentials are logged.

2. How we use information

  • To perform requested actions in your QuickBooks Online company (e.g., import COA, run reports).
  • To monitor and improve reliability and security of the Service.

3. Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • Data is shared only with QuickBooks Online APIs as required to perform the tasks you authorize.

4. Security

  • Keep your OAuth credentials and refresh tokens secure and rotate them if you suspect compromise.
  • Restrict access to the systems where the Service runs.

5. Data retention

  • Operational logs are retained only as long as needed for support and compliance, then deleted or anonymized.
  • You may request deletion of non-essential logs by contacting support.

6. Your choices

  • You can revoke QuickBooks access at any time via QuickBooks Online or by removing stored tokens.
  • Do not grant permissions you do not intend to use.

7. Children

The Service is for business use only and not directed to children under 16.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

Questions or requests? Email privacy@netmaxpass.com.
