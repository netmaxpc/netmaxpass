Disconnect NetMaxPass

If you want to revoke access:

  1. In QuickBooks Online, go to Settings > Apps > Authorized apps and disconnect NetMaxPass.
  2. Alternatively, contact support@netmaxpass.com and we will remove any stored tokens.
